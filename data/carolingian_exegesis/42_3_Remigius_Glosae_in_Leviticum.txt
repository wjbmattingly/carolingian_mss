42.3. [Remigius of Auxerre] Glosae in Leviticum.
Glosses | Leviticus
Manuscripts:
1. The Hague, Museum Meermanno-Westreenianum 10.A.7, f. 33va-35rb. saec. xii.*
2. Munich, Bayerische Staatsbibliothek Clm 3704, f. 86v-91v. saec. xi. *
3. Paris, Bibliothèque nationale n.a.l. 762, f. 68v-71v. saec. x. P: Trier, St. Maximin. *
4. Reims, Bibliothèque municipale 451, f. 145r-149v. saec. xii. St. Thierry, Reims.*
Attribution to Remigius based on weak circumstantial evidence. Haimo of Auxerre or Israel Scottus could also have written this commentary, or it could just be an anonymous late-Carolingian product, influenced by the school of Auxerre.
