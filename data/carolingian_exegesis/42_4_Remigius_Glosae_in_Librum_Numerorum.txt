Manuscripts:

4. Reims, Bibliothèque municipale, 451, f. 149v-154v. saec. xii. St. Thierry, Reims.*
Attribution to Remigius based on weak circumstantial evidence. Haimo of Auxerre or Israel Scottus could also have written this commentary, or it could just be an anonymous late-Carolingian product, influenced by the school of Auxerre.
